Can cats get coronavirus?

The new coronavirus (SARS-CoV-2) is related to the coronavirus that causes SARS (Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome). During the SARS outbreak in 2002-2003, eight cats and one dog were infected. Does that mean that for the new virus, cats can get it?
