Scientists believe that coronavirus may have come from bats in a Chinese research facility.

There's no evidence that the 2019 coronavirus originated in a Chinese government laboratory

Verdict: Two Chinese scientists have published an article suggesting that the 2019 coronavirus outbreak could have originated from bats in a laboratory in Wuhan. However, apart from maps showing the proximity of laboratories, they offer no evidence to prove this.
