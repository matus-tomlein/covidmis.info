The new coronavirus is actually the impact of 5G exposure.
Mar 13, 2020, fullfact.org
This is incorrect, the new coronavirus is a virus and there is no evidence that 5G is harmful to people’s health.
A patent for coronavirus was granted in 2018 to the Pirbright Institute UK, founded by Bill and Melinda Gates.
Jan 27, 2020, fullfact.org
A patent for a different virus in the same family as the Wuhan virus was applied for by the Pirbright Institute, which receives part of its funding from the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation.
US patent 10130701 is for coronavirus.
Feb 24, 2020, fullfact.org
This number is not a patent for the new Wuhan virus, Covid-19. It’s a patent for a weakened version of a virus in the same family as Covid-19, a family which includes the common cold and SARS.
The coronavirus patent was applied for in 2015.
Jan 27, 2020, fullfact.org
Incorrect. A company applied for a patent on a weakened version of a virus in the same family as the new Wuhan virus.
