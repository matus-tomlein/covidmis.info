A peer-reviewed research paper says that the new coronavirus has been artificially engineered.

A Norweigan-British research paper doesn’t claim the virus causing Covid-19 was man-made

Verdict: A Norweigan virologist has made claims about the non-natural origins of the new coronavirus. But this claim is not in a new peer-reviewed paper he co-authored. The scientific community widely agrees that the virus was not artificially engineered.
