Coronaviruses can live on surfaces for up to 28 days, so you should be careful if you receive a package from China.

There’s no evidence that the new coronavirus can survive on packages for 28 days

Verdict: There is currently no evidence that this virus can survive on surfaces that long. Most other coronaviruses do not. Current health advice suggests the risk of contracting the virus from a package is very low.
