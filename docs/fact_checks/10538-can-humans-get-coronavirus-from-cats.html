Covid-19 can be transmitted between humans and pets.
Mar 11, 2020, fullfact.org
There is one reported case of a pet dog in Hong Kong testing ‘weak positive’ for Covid-19. However, the dog is showing no symptoms and there is some question to whether the positive test is valid. The WHO and NHS says there is no evidence that pets can become infected or pass the virus to people.
Vets say that all cats should be kept indoors during the coronavirus outbreak.
Apr 08, 2020, fullfact.org
This advice only applies to cats from infected or self-isolating households. The article saying it applied to all cats has now been changed.
Coronavirus isn’t dangerous as it can be killed by Dettol products.
Feb 17, 2020, fullfact.org
How dangerous the novel coronavirus is doesn’t relate to whether disinfectants work against it. Coronavirus is a large category of viruses that includes the common cold. Dettol has said its products haven’t been tested against the new virus yet.
