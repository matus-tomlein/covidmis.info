Boris Johnson said that an alert system for Covid-19 could involve detecting the infection in the water supply.

Boris Johnson mistakenly said that Covid-19 could be monitored through the water supply

Verdict: It is true that Boris Johnson said this, however, he misspoke according to his spokesperson. He was referring to a potential scheme that could detect genetic material from the virus in sewage: not in the water supply.
