A majority of 6,200 doctors said hydroxychloroquine is the most effective coronavirus treatment.
Apr 08, 2020, fullfact.org
The poll the data comes from is not representative of all doctors. 37%, not a majority, of those involved in Covid-19 treatment worldwide said hydroxychloroquine was among the most effective treatments.
Hydroxychloroquine and chloroquine are cures for the new coronavirus.
Mar 27, 2020, fullfact.org
There aren’t yet peer-reviewed clinical studies to prove either drug is effective against the symptoms of, or can protect against, the new coronavirus. You should not take either unless you’re specifically prescribed them by a doctor.
The NIH researched chloroquine and concluded that it was effective at stopping the SARS coronavirus in its tracks

Incorrect
All seven patients who were getting treated for Covid-19 at Safdarjung hospital in New Delhi have recovered.
Mar 30, 2020, fullfact.org
There is a news story reporting that seven patients in this hospital in New Delhi had recovered. However, these were not the only Covid-19 patients in the city.
Doctors in India have been successful in treating coronavirus with a combination of drugs (Lopinavir, Retonovir, Oseltamivir along with Chlorphenamine) and are going to suggest the same medicine globally.
Mar 30, 2020, fullfact.org
India’s ministry of health has advised that the anti-HIV drugs, Lopinavir and Retonovir, are used in some groups of Covid-19 patients. But it is unclear how successful this treatment has been. The other two drugs from the claim are not mentioned in their guidance.
