Bill Gates created the new coronavirus and a vaccine for it.
Mar 13, 2020, fullfact.org
This is incorrect. The Bill Gates and Melinda Gates Foundation has funded a research institute that has a patent to a different coronavirus. Coronavirus is a large category of viruses that includes the common cold, SARS and the new virus that causes Covid-19.
A coronavirus vaccine for cows shows that the virus behind the current outbreak is not new.
Mar 10, 2020, fullfact.org
This is a different coronavirus from SARS-CoV-2, the virus that causes Covid-19. Coronaviruses are a large family of viruses that can affect a range of species.
Scientists knew about the new coronavirus long ago and called it “Disease X”.
Feb 18, 2020, fullfact.org
They didn’t. This is a misunderstanding about the phrase “Disease X”, which the WHO used to denote an unknown serious new disease that might cause a pandemic.
