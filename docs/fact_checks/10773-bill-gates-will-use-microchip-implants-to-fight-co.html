Bill Gates created the new coronavirus and a vaccine for it.
Mar 13, 2020, fullfact.org
This is incorrect. The Bill Gates and Melinda Gates Foundation has funded a research institute that has a patent to a different coronavirus. Coronavirus is a large category of viruses that includes the common cold, SARS and the new virus that causes Covid-19.
A patent for coronavirus was granted in 2018 to the Pirbright Institute UK, founded by Bill and Melinda Gates.
Jan 27, 2020, fullfact.org
A patent for a different virus in the same family as the Wuhan virus was applied for by the Pirbright Institute, which receives part of its funding from the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation.

A vaccine for the new coronavirus has been created by US scientists and will be ready to use from next Sunday.
Mar 26, 2020, fullfact.org
Whilst there are vaccines in development, it will likely be more than 18 months before they are ready to use. The image and videos accompanying the post are related to Covid-19 diagnostic tests, not vaccines.
Coronavirus may have been genetically engineered for “efficient spreading in the human population,” a bombshell new study has claimed.
Mar 11, 2020, fullfact.org
This is wrong and the study does not claim the new coronavirus has been genetically engineered. It simply compares the genome sequence of SARS-CoV-2 with other coronaviruses, and suggests a reason why it might be spreading relatively efficiently.
