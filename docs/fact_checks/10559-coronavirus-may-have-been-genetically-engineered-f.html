A study has not claimed the new coronavirus was ‘genetically engineered for efficient spread in humans’

Verdict: This is wrong and the study does not claim the new coronavirus has been genetically engineered. It simply compares the genome sequence of SARS-CoV-2 with other coronaviruses, and suggests a reason why it might be spreading relatively efficiently.
