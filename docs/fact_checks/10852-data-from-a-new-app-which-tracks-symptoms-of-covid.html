CovidMis.info
About

Data from a new app which tracks symptoms of Covid-19 claims that up to 6.6 million people in the UK have the disease.

We don’t know how many people in the UK have Covid-19 and the symptom tracker app doesn’t tell us

Verdict: This data cannot be extrapolated to the UK population. The number of people with Covid-19 may be higher, lower, or the same as claimed.

Jun 26, 2020, fullfact.org
This was the correct number of Covid-19 deaths (but only among hospital patients) where no pre-existing condition was reported, up to 26 May. Covid-19 death was determined by a positive test result or where Covid-19 was mentioned on the death certificate.
