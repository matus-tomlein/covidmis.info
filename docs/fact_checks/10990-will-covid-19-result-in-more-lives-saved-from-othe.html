Will COVID-19 result in more lives saved from other infectious diseases?

With washing hands, hygiene and social distancing now the new normal due to COVID-19, will this result in many lives saved from other infectious diseases like the flu or food-born illnesses (lower transmission). If so, would it be possible for the lives saved to be greater than the lives lost during COVID-19? Thanks.

Verdict: Negative
